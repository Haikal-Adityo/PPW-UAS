About Green Leaf

Welcome to Green Leaf, an Indonesian matcha tea company that was founded in 1990. We are dedicated to bringing you the finest Japanese matcha tea that originates from the pristine tea gardens of Japan.With a deep-rooted passion for matcha and its rich cultural heritage, Green Leaf was established to share the exquisite flavors and health benefits of this traditional Japanese tea with tea enthusiasts in Indonesia and beyond.

At Green Leaf, we take pride in carefully selecting the highest quality matcha leaves, harvested from shaded tea plants in Japan. Our matcha is expertly crafted using time-honored techniques, ensuring that each sip delivers a harmonious blend of vibrant color, smooth texture, and unparalleled flavor.

As a company deeply committed to sustainability, we prioritize environmentally friendly practices throughout our operations. From the responsible sourcing of our tea leaves to our eco-friendly packaging choices, we strive to make a positive impact on both the tea industry and the planet.

Experience the essence of Green Leaf and immerse yourself in the captivating world of matcha tea. Discover the rejuvenating power and mindful tranquility that our premium matcha brings to every cup. Join us on this remarkable journey as we celebrate the timeless art of matcha tea.

"At Green Leaf, we are dedicated to bringing you the finest matcha tea from Japan. Our passion for quality and sustainability drives us to deliver a truly authentic and enriching tea experience." - The Green Leaf Team

Production

At Green Leaf, we take pride in our meticulous matcha production process. From the tea gardens to the final product, we ensure every step is carried out with utmost care and expertise. Here's a glimpse into our matcha production:

1. Tea Harvest: Our skilled farmers carefully handpick the finest tea leaves from the shaded tea plants.

2. Processing: The freshly harvested leaves undergo meticulous processing to preserve their quality and flavor.

3. Grinding: The processed leaves are stone-ground into a vibrant green powder, resulting in our premium matcha.


Through our dedication to excellence and traditional techniques, we deliver matcha tea of exceptional taste and quality. Experience the journey of our matcha production and the artistry behind each cup.
